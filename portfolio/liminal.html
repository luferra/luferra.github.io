Concept & Artistic Methodology

Research-Driven Approach:

  • Taboo Deconstruction: Investigated BDSM beyond pornographic tropes through archival research (Instagram subcultures, fetish photography).
  • Sensory Semiotics: Selected materials (latex, rope, feathers) as tactile metaphors for power, restraint, and intimacy.
  • AI-Augmented Curation: Trained custom Stable Diffusion models on niche fetish archives to generate 200+ abstract visuals.
  • Iterative prompt engineering: Shifted from literal ("bondage poses") to atmospheric ("desire through chiaroscuro").

Technical Process

Prototyping Physical Interaction:

  • Tested 3 sensor types (accelerometers, proximity, vibration) with material samples.
  • Final Solution: Ultrasonic sensors in 3D-printed mounts for 360° responsiveness.

Spatial Design

Welded iron cages with material-wrapped interiors (tactile differentiation).

Site-Specific Adaptation

Designed for Torre dei Forni’s industrial concrete aesthetics.

Multimedia Integration

Raspberry Pi/Arduino system triggered by proximity to display AI visuals + ambient soundscapes.

Interaction Logic

Partial torso insertion = consent metaphor.

Key Artistic Decisions

  • Avoiding Literalism: No explicit imagery; abstract textures and shadows.
  • Material Contrast: Rough iron vs. soft feathers to mirror BDSM’s duality of pain/pleasure.
  • Sound Design: Sub-bass frequencies to evoke physiological tension.

Exhibition & Impact

  • Visitor Experience: 78% engaged beyond 5 minutes (observed); 62% returned for repeat interaction.
  • Critical Response: Praised for "reclaiming kink as contemplative art" (Local Culture Review).
