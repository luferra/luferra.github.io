4eu+ Student Portal

UX research, UX design

The 4EU+ European University Alliance is a transnational strategic association of European Universities.

The Student Portal serves as a digital platform that paves the way for a virtual transnational campus, designed to be open and inclusive for all members of the student community from partner institutions interested in engaging with the **4EU+ Alliance**.

What I did

  • User research
  • UX design
  • Prototyping and wireframing
  • User testing

Visit Website

The overall creative activity

The design process started in 2022 and concluded in December of the same year with the launch of the initial version of the Student Portal. We began from the ground up, starting only with a request for a tool that would allow students and teachers to access learning opportunities provided by various partners within the Alliance.

The initial activities, which I led under the coordination of the University of Copenhagen, concentrated on identifying the key features of local Student Portals with an emphasis on student needs.

We employed a Design Thinking approach throughout this process.
